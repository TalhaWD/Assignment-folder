Production

In 2018, it was reported that Ajay Devgn will produce the film with Abhishek Bachchan playing the lead role.[4] The film is directed by Kookie Gulati and shows the financial crimes and life of Harshad Mehta. Nikita Dutta and Ileana D'Cruz were brought in to play supporting roles. [8]

The shooting commenced on 16 September 2019. [9] Many shots of the movie has been filmed in Gautam Buddha University. [10]


Release

Due to the COVID-19 pandemic , the film was released for streaming worldwide from 8 April 2021 on Hotstar. [6]
